GET INVOLVED IN THE ART WORLD

Take this opportunity to join us and help us expand the world of art. We offer an opportunity to enter the art world whether it is as an artist, through sponsering us or even as an intern just looking for some experience. We welcome the right mindset with open arms

Submitting your artwork

Contact us to book an appointment( for artists in South Africa,United States,France,Spain,Nigeria)before delivering your artwork to the nearest Artworld facility near you. For artists with no access to our facility please send an application.

Volunteering

We greatly appreciate any type of assistance because the world of art is growing at an exponential rate. This would also offer valuable experience to anyone tring to learn about the art industry.
